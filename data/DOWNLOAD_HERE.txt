Dataset: Instacart Market Basket Analysis
Source: Kaggle
Link: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/yasserh/instacart-online-grocery-basket-analysis-dataset

Instructions:
1. Download all CSV files from the link above
2. Save them in the data/raw/ folder:
   - aisles.csv
   - departments.csv
   - order_products__prior.csv
   - order_products__train.csv
   - orders.csv
   - products.csv
3. Run the notebook at notebooks/retail_analytics.ipynb
4. The Parquet files and DuckDB warehouse are generated automatically
